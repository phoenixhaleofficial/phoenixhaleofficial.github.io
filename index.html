About Me

Hi there! 👋 I'm Phoenix, a hobbyist programmer, and founder of the upcoming Alternative Rock band Monochrome Resonance, formed in 2024. I'm passionate about creating music that resonates with people's emotions.

Monochrome Resonance

Monochrome Resonance is an Alternative Rock band founded in 2024.

Our music draws inspiration from Linkin Park. We're currently working new material, which showcases our unique blend of electronic and rock elements.

Musical Journey

In February 2023, I began searching for band members, creating flyers and attending the Music Camp 2023, where I gained a significant following on Instagram.

In April 2024, I met Jan Kinder, marking the beginning of Monochrome Resonance.
